Turn Economy

This page covers how Raidbound uses deterministic ATB pacing, charge-based decision making, and action costs to shape combat tempo without overwhelming the player.

Back to Home

Overview

The combat pace is meant to feel deliberate. Players are managing charges, timing, and opportunity cost, not just waiting for a single bar to fill and pressing the biggest button available.

Deterministic ATB

A cleaner action structure helps the game feel tactical instead of random, while still keeping forward combat motion.

Charge Economy

Abilities live inside a limited action economy, making timing and cost part of every decision.

Pacing Goals

The system aims for readable pressure, not pure speed. Clarity matters more than spectacle for its own sake.

UI Support

Gauges, glow states, and action presentation reinforce when a unit is ready and what the next meaningful choice is.

What this page communicates

  • Raidbound has a combat identity beyond generic turn-based labels.
  • The ATB layer is a system with intent, not just a visual flourish.
  • The project reflects structured design thinking and tuning discipline.
