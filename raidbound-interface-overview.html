Overview

Raidbound leans hard into readable, high-contrast UI. The interface is meant to present a lot of information without looking like a debugging tool or spreadsheet pasted onto the screen.

Combat Card Layout

Enemy and hero cards carry the tactical information load and need to remain readable at a glance.

Panel Language

Consistent borders, spacing, and screen framing help the game feel authored and cohesive.

Collection Screens

Hero and item collections are part showcase, part progression dashboard, and part UX proof point.

Readability First

Every layer is evaluated through clarity: what the player needs, what can wait, and what should never be buried.
