About Me

I’m a solo developer with 8 years in the gaming industry working in production and player support. I’m now focused on building smooth, player-first game systems through independent projects.

My primary goal right now is to polish Raidbound into a fun, replayable experience that I’m proud of. Once the first two acts are fully realized and engaging, I’ll consider that a major personal milestone. Long-term, I aim to release Raidbound on Steam so others can experience and enjoy the game.

Projects

Raidbound (Godot 4.6)

A tactical roguelite RPG focused on party management, UI clarity, and a unique combat experience. To showcase that combat, Raidbound has hit breaks, floating damage numbers, glowing ATB gauges, attacking & healing with threat generation, and dozens of hero/enemy abilities with buffs/debuffs.

Status: In active development. Public demo in progress.

Fractals (Interactive Visualizer Prototype)

A live visual system built to respond not only to music, but to gameplay energy, combat pacing, player actions, and cinematic transitions. This project is part visualizer, part rendering sandbox, and part prototype for future game mechanics.

I am exploring how a reactive visual layer can make a game feel more alive: boss phases can escalate the scene, menus can breathe, hits can distort space, and audio can push the entire screen into a new state.

Status: Live prototype hosted on the site. Best experienced in fullscreen with Demo Mode, Mic, or System Audio.

  • Audio-reactive
  • Gameplay-reactive
  • Fullscreen web experience
  • Prototype for future games
